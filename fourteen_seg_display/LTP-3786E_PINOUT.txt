LTP-3786E 14-Segment Display Pinout
====================================

The LTP-3786E is a 2-digit 14-segment common anode display in an 18-pin DIP package.


TOP VIEW (looking down at the display face):
=============================================

              Display Face
         ┌────────────────────┐
         │  [Digit1] [Digit2] │  ← You see the segments here
         └────────────────────┘
                   │
                   ▼
    Pin 1  ●──────────────────●  Pin 18
    Pin 2  ●──────────────────●  Pin 17
    Pin 3  ●──────────────────●  Pin 16
    Pin 4  ●──────────────────●  Pin 15
    Pin 5  ●──────────────────●  Pin 14
    Pin 6  ●──────────────────●  Pin 13
    Pin 7  ●──────────────────●  Pin 12
    Pin 8  ●──────────────────●  Pin 11
    Pin 9  ●──────────────────●  Pin 10
           └──────────────────┘
         Left Side    Right Side


BOTTOM VIEW (looking up from PCB, pins pointing toward you):
=============================================================

           ┌──────────────────┐
    Pin 9  ●──────────────────●  Pin 10
    Pin 8  ●──────────────────●  Pin 11
    Pin 7  ●──────────────────●  Pin 12
    Pin 6  ●──────────────────●  Pin 13
    Pin 5  ●──────────────────●  Pin 14
    Pin 4  ●──────────────────●  Pin 15
    Pin 3  ●──────────────────●  Pin 16
    Pin 2  ●──────────────────●  Pin 17
    Pin 1  ●──────────────────●  Pin 18
                   │
                   ▼
         ┌────────────────────┐
         │  [Digit1] [Digit2] │  ← Display face (other side)
         └────────────────────┘
         Left Side    Right Side


PIN FUNCTIONS:
==============

LEFT SIDE (Pins 1-9):          RIGHT SIDE (Pins 10-18):
Pin 1:   E                     Pin 18:  K
Pin 2:   D2                    Pin 17:  J
Pin 3:   CA1 (Digit 1 Anode)   Pin 16:  H
Pin 4:   C                     Pin 15:  I
Pin 5:   DP1 (Decimal Pt 1)    Pin 14:  D1
Pin 6:   B                     Pin 13:  CA2 (Digit 2 Anode)
Pin 7:   A2                    Pin 12:  DP2 (Decimal Pt 2)
Pin 8:   A1                    Pin 11:  G1
Pin 9:   F                     Pin 10:  G2


COMPLETE PIN TABLE:
===================

Pin | Function | Description                        | Connect to FPGA
----|----------|------------------------------------|-----------------
1   | E        | Segment E (bottom left vertical)   | SEG[6] + 330Ω
2   | D2       | Segment D2 (bottom right half)     | SEG[5] + 330Ω
3   | CA1      | Common Anode Digit 1 (LEFT digit)  | DIG[1] or DIG[3]*
4   | C        | Segment C (bottom right vertical)  | SEG[3] + 330Ω
5   | DP1      | Decimal Point 1 (not used)         | N/C
6   | B        | Segment B (top right vertical)     | SEG[2] + 330Ω
7   | A2       | Segment A2 (top right half)        | SEG[1] + 330Ω
8   | A1       | Segment A1 (top left half)         | SEG[0] + 330Ω
9   | F        | Segment F (top left vertical)      | SEG[7] + 330Ω
10  | G2       | Segment G2 (middle right half)     | SEG[9] + 330Ω
11  | G1       | Segment G1 (middle left half)      | SEG[8] + 330Ω
12  | DP2      | Decimal Point 2 (not used)         | N/C
13  | CA2      | Common Anode Digit 2 (RIGHT digit) | DIG[0] or DIG[2]*
14  | D1       | Segment D1 (bottom left half)      | SEG[4] + 330Ω
15  | I        | Segment I (middle top vertical)    | SEG[11] + 330Ω
16  | H        | Segment H (middle bottom vertical) | SEG[10] + 330Ω
17  | J        | Segment J (top-right diagonal)     | SEG[12] + 330Ω
18  | K        | Segment K (bottom-right diagonal)  | SEG[13] + 330Ω

* Display 1: DIG[0]=rightmost, DIG[1]=leftmost
* Display 2: DIG[2]=rightmost, DIG[3]=leftmost


14-SEGMENT LAYOUT (Each Digit):
================================

           A1    A2
          ───I   ───
         │    │ │    │
        F│  J │ │ K  │B
         │    │ │    │
          ─G1─   ─G2─
         │    │ │    │
        E│  H │ │    │C
         │    │ │    │
          ───    ───
           D1    D2


IDENTIFYING PIN 1:
==================
- Pin 1 is marked with a dot or notch on the package
- With display face UP and text readable, Pin 1 is TOP-LEFT
- Pins count counter-clockwise from Pin 1 (top view)


EXAMPLE: Inserting into Breadboard (Bottom View)
=================================================

When you insert the display into a breadboard, you're looking at the
BOTTOM view. The pin numbers are:

           Row 1: 9  8  7  6  5  4  3  2  1
                  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓  ↓
                 ┌──────────────────────────┐
                 │    LTP-3786E             │
                 │  [Digit1] [Digit2]       │← Display face (other side)
                 └──────────────────────────┘
                  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑  ↑
           Row 2: 10 11 12 13 14 15 16 17 18


WIRING SUMMARY:
===============
All 14 segment pins need 220-330Ω current-limiting resistors!

Display 1 (4 digits total, this is digits 0-1):
  Pin 3  (CA1) → DIG[1]  (left digit of display 1)
  Pin 13 (CA2) → DIG[0]  (right digit of display 1)

Display 2 (4 digits total, this is digits 2-3):
  Pin 3  (CA1) → DIG[3]  (left digit of display 2)
  Pin 13 (CA2) → DIG[2]  (right digit of display 2)

All segment pins (1,2,4,6,7,8,9,10,11,14,15,16,17,18) connect to
SEG[0-13] through resistors - these are SHARED between both displays.
